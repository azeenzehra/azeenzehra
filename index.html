About me

I am a scientist who likes to think with experiments and write with clarity. I enjoy taking complicated ideas in biology and turning them into stories that students, researchers and curious readers can actually use.

This page collects my work: research, protocols, science writing, books I love, and a few things I am quietly very proud of.
